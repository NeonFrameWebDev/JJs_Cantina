Our Story

A Rocky Point Legend

JJ's Cantina didn't just open in Cholla Bay. It helped put Rocky Point on the map. Since 1984, this waterfront landmark has been the gathering place for locals, expats, and visitors from across the border who know that the best view of the Sea of Cortez comes with a cold margarita in hand.

Perched on the tiered stone terraces above the bay, JJ's is everything a cantina should be: unpretentious, welcoming, and alive. The walls are lined with decades of memories. The bartenders have poured a few thousand sunsets. The kitchen keeps it real with fresh, honest food that belongs right here on the water.

Recently renovated and better than ever, JJ's carries its history proudly while looking toward the future. Same legendary soul. Brand new experience.

Entertainment

Live Music Under the Sonoran Sky

The open-air stage at JJ's is one of a kind. Every weekend, multiple bands take the stage as the sun drops into the Sea of Cortez behind them, painting the sky orange, purple, and gold. There is no better soundtrack to a Rocky Point evening.

Whether you are dancing on the patio or settling into a stone chair with a cold drink, the music at JJ's is not background noise. It is the main event.
